Overview

Infosec Team’s intentions for publishing an Acceptable Use Policy is not to impose restrictions that are contrry to CRH’s established culture of openness, trust and integrity. CRH is committe to protecting CRH's employees partners and the company from illegal or damaging actions by individuals, either knowingly or unknowingly .

Internet/Intranet/Extranet-related systems, including but not limited to computer equipment, mobile device, software, operating system, storage media, network accounts providing electronic mail, WWW browsing, and FTP, are the property of CRH. These systems are to be used for business purpose in serving the interests of the company, and of our clients and customers during normal operations. Please review Human Resources policies for further details.

Effective security is a team effort involving the participation and support of every CRH employee and affiliate who deals with information and/or information systems. It is the responsibility of every computer user to know these guideline, and to conduct their activitie accordingly.

Purpose

The purpose of this policy is to outline the acceptable use of computer equipment and other electronic devices at CRH. These rules are in place to protect the employee and CRH. Inappropriate use exposes CRH to cyber risks including virus attacks including ransomware, compromise of network systems and services, data breach, and legal issues.

Scope

This policy applies to the use of information, electronic and computing devices, and network resources to conduct CRH business or interact with internal networks and business systems, whether owned or leased by CRH, the employee, or a third party. All employees, contractors, consultants, temporary, and other workers at CRH and its subsidiaries are responsible for exercising good judgment regarding appropriate use of information, electronic devices, and network resources in accordance with CRH policies and standards, and local laws and regulation. Exceptions to this policy are documented in section 5.2

This policy applies to employees, contractors, consultants, temporaries, and other workers at CRH, including all personnel affiliated with third parties. This policy applies to all equipment that is owned or leased by CRH.
